What Is The RollBall

Rollball is a fast-paced team sport that combines elements of basketball, handball, and roller skating.
Concept
Roll Ball is a game played between two teams. Roll Ball is played on “ROLLER SHOES”. Each team consists of 12 (Twelve) players, 6(six) on the field and 6(Six) in reserve,5 field players in defence of 2-3 and 1 goalkeeper. The main objective of the game is to score maximum goals within a stipulated time. The main feature of Roll Ball is that the ball is held in single or both the hands, even when passing on to the other players, and is repeatedly bounced on the ground.

Pillars of Rollball

The game stands on 4 pillars: Balance, Speed, Accuracy, and Team Work — essential for every athlete.

Speed
Speed is a vital component of this sport, as it enables players to create scoring opportunities and chase down opponents.
Balance
Balance is a crucial aspect of sports performance. In many sports, athletes need to maintain their balance while moving quickly and changing direction.
Accuracy
To develop accuracy, athletes must develop the technical skills and physical attributes needed to execute precise movements.
Team Work
Teamwork involves effective communication, trust, and collaboration among team members to achieve a common goal.

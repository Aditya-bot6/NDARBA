What Is The RollBall

Rollball is a fast-paced team sport that combines elements of basketball, handball, and roller skating.
Concept
Roll Ball is a game played between two teams. Roll Ball is played on ‚ÄúROLLER SHOES‚Äù. Each team consists of 12 (Twelve) players, 6(six) on the field and 6(Six) in reserve,5 field players in defence of 2-3 and 1 goalkeeper. The main objective of the game is to score maximum goals within a stipulated time. The main feature of Roll Ball is that the ball is held in single or both the hands, even when passing on to the other players, and is repeatedly bounced on the ground.

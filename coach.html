Coaching

Coaching At Roll Ball Nashik, we believe in building strong fundamentals, discipline, and team spirit through professional coaching and regular practice. Our coaching program is designed for players of all levels – from beginners to advanced athletes. Whether you're just starting out or want to sharpen your skills for competitive matches, our experienced coaches and senior players will guide you at every step.

What We Offer:

Structured Training Sessions

Regular practice focusing on skating, ball control, passing, defending, and strategy.

Team Development

We train as a unit to build coordination and match readiness. Our team represents Nashik in various tournaments.

Skill Building

Individual attention to each player to help improve their strengths and work on weak areas.

Beginner-Friendly

New to roll ball? No problem! We’ll help you learn the basics and get comfortable on the court.

Who Can Join:

Boys and girls aged 6 and above

Beginners, intermediates, and advanced players

Anyone passionate about Roll Ball and committed to learning
